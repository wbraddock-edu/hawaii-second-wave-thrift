Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Second Wave Thrift.

We're open Monday through Saturday 10am–6pm and Sunday 11am–4pm. On Friday Art Night, we extend our hours until 9pm. We're open most holidays but close for Thanksgiving and Christmas Day.
New items are processed and put out daily. Our biggest restocking days are Tuesday and Friday, when we process the week's donations and estate pickups. If you're a serious treasure hunter, those are the best days to visit.
Yes! We accept gently used clothing, shoes, accessories, furniture, home goods, books, records, and collectibles. Please ensure items are clean and functional. We cannot accept: heavily damaged or moldy items, mattresses, large appliances, or broken electronics. When in doubt, call us first.
Yes, we offer free furniture pickup for qualifying donations within 10 miles of Hanapepe. This is for larger furniture items or estate cleanouts. Please call ahead or use our online form to schedule — we need at least 3 days notice for pickups.
Absolutely. We issue donation receipts at the time of drop-off. For large estate donations, we can provide a detailed itemized receipt. We're a registered 501(c)(3) organization, so all donations are tax-deductible. Keep your receipt for your records.
Yes! Every Friday evening we stay open late until 9pm for Hanapepe Art Night. It's one of our favorite events of the week — we put out special items for Art Night, and the combination of gallery hopping and thrift browsing is a quintessential Hanapepe experience.
We can arrange shipping for smaller items like clothing and books. For larger items and furniture, we can coordinate local delivery within Kauai but cannot ship off-island. Contact us before you visit if you're a visitor who wants to ensure shipping is possible for a specific type of item.
We do accept consignment for higher-value vintage items — particularly valuable vintage aloha shirts, authentic Hawaiian collectibles, and fine art. Our consignment split is 50/50. Email us photos of what you'd like to consign and we'll let you know if it's a good fit for our shop.
For exceptional vintage finds — rare aloha shirts, authentic vintage Hawaiian jewelry, and significant collectibles — we sometimes offer outright purchase. This is evaluated case by case. Bring the item in and we'll take a look.
